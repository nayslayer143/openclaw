間 Ma · Negative Space

Every dish is a meditation — silence rendered edible, darkness made beautiful.

KURO was born from the Japanese concept of Ma — the profound meaning found in emptiness. Our kaiseki menu traces the arc of a single night: from the first hesitant light to the deep silence that follows. We use only what the season offers. Nothing more. Nothing less.
