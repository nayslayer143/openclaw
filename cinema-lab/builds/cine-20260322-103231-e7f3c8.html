// About the Artist

HXLF

Berlin-based producer Felix Hahn performs as HXLF — a project built around the collision between industrial texture and melodic desolation. His music doesn't resolve. It accumulates. Drawn from late nights in the city's eastern edges, every track is a document of a specific, unrepeatable atmosphere.
