01

Type is the hero. If you remove all color and images and it still works, you've won.

02

Tension is beauty. Perfect symmetry is boring. Near-symmetry is electric.

03

Whitespace is not empty. It's pressurized. It pushes elements apart with intent.

04

Motion has meaning. If you can't explain why it moves, it shouldn't.

05

Break your own rules. Every rule exists to be broken — once you understand why it exists.

06

The screen is not paper. Use viewport, scroll, pointer, time. The medium is dynamic.

07

Details at every zoom level. A great design rewards the glance AND the stare.

08

Steal like an artist. Study Müller-Brockmann, Carson, Sagmeister, Toda. Then make it yours.
